ACTIVITIES






Zorbing

Have some splashing fun indulging in zorbing at Puzzles.Water Zorbing involves a water ball or water walking ball which is a large inflatable sphere that allows a person inside it to walk across the surface of a body of water. This 10 minute activity promises to give you the giggles as you try to meander through the water surface.

Ropewalk

A ropewalk is a long straight narrow lane, or a covered pathway, where long strands of material are laid before being twisted into rope. Due to the length of some ropewalks, workers may use bicycles to get from one end to the other.

Zip Lining

One of the special rides you would enjoy here is that Zip line ride. Don't miss ... Zip line are very designed nicely with safety feature. ..... The best of zip lining at Puzzles!





Quad Biking

If you love the rugged road and all the thrills and adventures that it offers you, then you need to head for an exciting quad biking experience and explore the rough mud and rocky roads that lie ahead of you! Revv up your engines and head for an exciting offbeat quad biking ride in Puzzles.

Rappelling

Rappelling in Puzzles is an extreme adventure activity that literally keeps you on your toes and feet. The camper is in total control of their movements while descending from a rocky cliff. The activity has gained a lot of thrill seekers and is performed under the strict safety standards of instructors.

Camping

Free from city noise, technology and other modern interferences, The Solitude Camp is an undisturbed and peaceful place in the Puzzles.
