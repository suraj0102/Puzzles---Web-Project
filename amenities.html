AMENITIES

---------Swimming Pool---------

Grand Pool : A luxurious pool with attached four Jacuzzis and twelve feet cave waterfalls. Enjoy a drink in the evenings at our bubble pool bar while in water

Party Pool : A Lazy pool just to refresh oneself with an integrated falls , music is played at the background.

Kids Pool : Kids love water , they can have a great time

Private Pool : Attached with the cottage a mini pool which can be used by the cottage occupied guests.

---------Room Service---------

Room service enabling our guests to choose cocktails or delicious menu items for delivery to their cottages at your convenience.

---------Wifi---------

We strongly recommend to leave behind the online world once you enter our resort to enjoy the nature in the best way.

Unless you have an important requirement to go online , then you can connect to the solar powered eco wifi in the rooms or restaurant or anywhere in the resort premises.

---------Sports---------

With concrete blocks taking over our city playing grounds , we have been restricted to only watch on television. A two acre ground for outdoor sports to play : Cricket , Football , Basket ball , Volley ball , Tug-of-war , Badminton and others .

Indoor Games : Table Tennis , Foosball table , Board games

---------Rain Dance---------

Dancing in a mist filled floor one of a unique experience, with great mix of tunes by our in house DJ.

---------Conference---------

At Puzzles Resort we really want you to leave behind the conferences at your office to enjoy the true nature , but just incase you have a meet for a presentation or a any function get together, you group can utilise our conference hall with a capacity upto 120 people with audio and video assist. Please enquire the availability before arrival.
